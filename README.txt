Welcome to the EGaDS 2022 Mega-Micro-jam! This is a one-of-a-kind game jam inspired by WarioWare style games: participants create micro-games that can be completed in just 4-8 seconds, and after the jam we combine everyoneâ€™s minigames into one big project for everyone to play!
You can find our previous Mega-Micro games on our itch: https://egads-austin.itch.io/

The jam will last from the **19th of December at 1 pm CT until 11:59 pm CT on December 23rd.**

All important annoucements will be sent out on the jam discord (found here: https://discord.gg/7MHcmY8R), so make sure to join it to stay updated!

Huge credit to Grant Ross, the EGaDS officer who spearheaded the first version of this jam in Fall 2020.

Before you start working on your micro game, please read through the contribution guidelines so that you may learn about the restrictions and guidelines that you must follow so that your game will work within our Mega Micro Framework.
The contribution guidelines can be found here: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1N26wcoxD5LoRngOQHNmapVGeILztsO_eL14ub_Fq5gc/edit?usp=sharing
These guidelines will show you how to make your own micro game, how to test it, and how to submit it. 

If you have any questions, send a message in #ask-the-officers or pm one of the officers!.

Happy Jamming!
